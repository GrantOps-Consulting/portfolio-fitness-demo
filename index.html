The Classes

Six classes,
one floor.

Every class capped at twelve so coaches can actually coach. Drop in for one, stack three a week if you've signed up for full membership, or train solo on the open floor.

01 · Striking

Boxing
Fundamentals

Footwork, jab-cross, slip and counter. The proper basics — done slow, then drilled fast. No prior experience needed.

Duration 60 Min Level Open
02 · Strength

Strength
& Power

Compound barbell work — squat, deadlift, bench, press, row. Coached programming, six racks, no waiting.

Duration 75 Min Level All
03 · Striking

Pad
Sessions

One-on-one or two-on-one with a coach. Combinations, defence, conditioning rounds. Booked separately.

Duration 30 Min Level All
04 · Cardio

HIIT
Conditioning

Bag rounds, burpees, sled, rower. Intervals built around boxing rounds — three on, one off. Bring water.

Duration 45 Min Level Any
05 · Open Floor

Open
Floor

Train on your own programme — racks, dumbbells, bags, mats. Coaches on the floor for form checks.

Duration 90 Min Level Self-Led
06 · Advanced

Sparring
(Approval)

Light to medium technical sparring, head coach in the ring. Approval-only — three months of fundamentals first.

Duration 60 Min Level Adv.

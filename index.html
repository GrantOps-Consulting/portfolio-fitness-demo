Round 01 / 07 — The Floor
The Floor

Eight years.
One floor.

BOXWORKS opened at the foot of Bridgeton Cross in 2018 — six chairs, six coaches, one philosophy: every session gets the time the work needs.

Boxing on one half of the floor, strength on the other. A real ring on a coach-only schedule. Twenty-eight classes a week capped at twelve so coaches can actually coach.
